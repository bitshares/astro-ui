**Portfolio Balances** shows every asset an account holds — free balance, frozen/vesting portions, and the asset's details. It's a read-only viewer: you can sort, search, and mark assets as favourites (stored locally in your browser).

**Open in the app:** [Portfolio Balances](/balances.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="All holdings" icon="wallet">
    List every asset and amount for an account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sort & search" icon="search">
    Filter by symbol, hide zero balances, sort by value.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Favourite assets" icon="star">
    Star assets for quick access (saved in your browser).
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Portfolio Balances** from the homepage under *Account Overviews*.

2. Enter or select the account whose balances you want to inspect.

3. Browse, sort and search the holdings.

4. Star frequently-used assets to add them to your local favourites.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="Local only">
  Favouriting an asset writes to your browser's local storage — it is not an on-chain action and is not shared between devices.
</Aside>
