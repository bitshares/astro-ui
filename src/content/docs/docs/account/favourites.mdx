**Favourites** is your personal quick-access hub. It stores favourite accounts, assets and trading pairs in your browser's local storage so you can jump back to them instantly. Nothing here touches the chain.

**Open in the app:** [Favourites](/favourites.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Accounts" icon="user">
    Pin frequently-used accounts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Assets" icon="gem">
    Pin assets you trade or hold.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pairs" icon="swap">
    Pin market pairs for one-click trading.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Favourites** from the homepage under *Account Overviews*.

2. Add accounts, assets and market pairs via their respective "favourite" controls elsewhere in the app — they appear here.

3. Click any entry to navigate straight to it.

4. Remove an entry to clear it from local storage.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="Per-device">
  Favourites live in your browser, so they aren't shared across devices or accounts and aren't on-chain.
</Aside>
