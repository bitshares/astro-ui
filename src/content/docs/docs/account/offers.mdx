**Credit Offers** lists credit offers in the marketplace — both the ones you've published as a lender and ones you can borrow from. It's a read-only browser; the actual create/accept/update happens on the Lend and offer pages.

**Open in the app:** [Credit Offers](/offers.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Your offers" icon="document">
    See the credit offers you've published.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Browse market" icon="search">
    Filter all offers by asset, fee and collateral.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Act" icon="arrow-right">
    Edit your offer, or proceed to accept someone's.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Credit Offers** from the homepage under *Account Overviews*.

2. Switch between your offers and the full marketplace.

3. Use **Edit** on your own offer (opens [Lend](/docs/debt/lend.html)) or **Proceed** on another (opens the offer accept flow).

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="No broadcast here">
  This is a browser. Publishing/accepting uses `credit_offer_create` / `credit_offer_accept` / `credit_offer_update`, documented on [Lend](/docs/debt/lend.html).
</Aside>
