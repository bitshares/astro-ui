**Pools** lists every liquidity pool on the chain with its two assets, share asset, reserves and fees. It's a read-only directory — open any pool to track it or jump into staking and swapping.

**Open in the app:** [Pools](/pools.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Full directory" icon="droplets">
    Every `1.19.x` liquidity pool.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stats" icon="chart">
    Reserves, share asset and fee percentages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Act" icon="arrow-right">
    Track, stake or swap from any pool.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Pools** from the homepage under *Blockchain Overviews*.

2. Search or filter the pool list.

3. Open a pool for detail, or go straight to [Stake](/docs/exchanging/stake.html) / [Swap](/docs/exchanging/swap.html).

</Steps>
