**Top Markets** ranks the busiest trading pairs on the DEX by volume and activity. It's a read-only leaderboard to discover liquid markets and jump straight into the [DEX](/docs/exchanging/dex.html).

**Open in the app:** [Top Markets](/top-markets.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Liquid pairs" icon="trending-up">
    The highest-volume markets on the DEX.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discovery" icon="search">
    Find tradable pairs quickly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trade" icon="arrow-right">
    Open any market on the DEX.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Top Markets** from the homepage under *Blockchain Overviews*.

2. Browse the ranked list of active markets.

3. Click a market to open it on the [DEX](/docs/exchanging/dex.html).

</Steps>
