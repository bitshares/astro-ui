**Top Pools** ranks liquidity pools by volume and activity — the busiest places to [Stake](/docs/exchanging/stake.html) and [Swap](/docs/exchanging/swap.html). It's a read-only leaderboard for discovering deep pools.

**Open in the app:** [Top Pools](/top-pools.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Deep pools" icon="trending-up">
    The highest-volume liquidity pools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discovery" icon="search">
    Find liquid pools to provide to.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Act" icon="arrow-right">
    Track, stake or swap from any pool.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Top Pools** from the homepage under *Blockchain Overviews*.

2. Browse the ranked list of active pools.

3. Open a pool to track it, or jump to [Stake](/docs/exchanging/stake.html) / [Swap](/docs/exchanging/swap.html).

</Steps>
