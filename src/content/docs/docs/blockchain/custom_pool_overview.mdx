**Custom Pool Tracker** focuses on one liquidity pool at a time. Given a pool id (`1.19.x`), it shows the two reserves, the share asset supply, the implied price and recent swap activity — a targeted view for a pool you care about.

**Open in the app:** [Custom Pool Tracker](/custom_pool_overview.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Single-pool focus" icon="droplets">
    Deep-dive one `1.19.x` pool.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reserves & price" icon="chart">
    See both reserves and the implied swap price.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share supply" icon="gem">
    Outstanding LP share tokens.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Custom Pool Tracker** from the homepage under *Blockchain Overviews*.

2. Enter or paste a pool id (`1.19.x`).

3. Review its reserves, share asset, price and activity.

4. Jump to [Stake](/docs/exchanging/stake.html) to deposit/withdraw, or [Swap](/docs/exchanging/swap.html) to trade.

</Steps>
