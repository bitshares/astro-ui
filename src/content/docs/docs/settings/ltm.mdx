**Lifetime Membership (LTM)** upgrades your account so you receive a 80% referral-fee rebate and, crucially, gain the ability to issue assets and do other privileged actions. Upgrading broadcasts a single `account_upgrade` with `upgrade_to_lifetime_member: true`.

**Open in the app:** [Lifetime Membership](/ltm.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Upgrade" icon="crown">
    Pay the LTM fee to upgrade your account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Issue assets" icon="gem">
    LTM is required to create UIAs/smartcoins.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rebates" icon="coins">
    Earn referral-fee rebates.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Lifetime Membership** from the homepage under *Settings*.

2. Confirm you have enough BTS to cover the LTM fee.

3. Authorize. The app broadcasts `account_upgrade`.

</Steps>

<Aside type="caution" title="One-way, paid">
  The upgrade is permanent and costs the network's LTM fee (paid in BTS). Make sure you have sufficient balance before signing.
</Aside>

## Operation used

LTM broadcasts `account_upgrade` (operation `8`):

```json
{
  "account_to_upgrade": "1.2.x",
  "upgrade_to_lifetime_member": true,
  "extensions": {}
}
```

| Field | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `account_to_upgrade` | Your account (`1.2.x`). |
| `upgrade_to_lifetime_member` | Must be `true` for LTM. |
