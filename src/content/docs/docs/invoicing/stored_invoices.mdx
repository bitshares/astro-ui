**Stored Invoices** is your local inbox of invoices you've received and saved. Like the rest of the invoicing feature, these are off-chain base58 strings kept in your browser — you can decode, review and open them for payment. Nothing here broadcasts.

**Open in the app:** [Stored Invoices](/stored_invoices.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Received inbox" icon="inbox">
    Invoices others have sent you, saved locally.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Decode & review" icon="document">
    Inspect items and totals before paying.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pay" icon="send">
    Send any invoice to [Pay Invoice](/docs/invoicing/pay_invoice.html).
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Stored Invoices** from the homepage under *Invoicing*.

2. Browse the invoices you've received and saved.

3. Open one to review its decoded contents.

4. Click **Pay** to settle it via [Pay Invoice](/docs/invoicing/pay_invoice.html), which broadcasts the `transfer`(s).

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="Per-device and off-chain">
  Stored invoices live only in your browser's local storage. The actual payment is a normal `transfer` documented on [Pay Invoice](/docs/invoicing/pay_invoice.html).
</Aside>
