**Committee** lists the committee members (`1.5.x`) who govern protocol parameters and worker approval. It's a read-only directory; cast your votes on the [Vote](/docs/governance/vote.html) page.

**Open in the app:** [Committee](/committee.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Member list" icon="users">
    All committee members with their stats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active status" icon="shield">
    Which members are currently active.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Votes" icon="gavel">
    Total votes each member has received.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Committee** from the homepage under *Governance*.

2. Browse the members and their standing.

3. To vote, open [Vote](/docs/governance/vote.html) and select them.

</Steps>
