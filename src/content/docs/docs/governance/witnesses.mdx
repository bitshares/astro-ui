**Witnesses** lists the block producers (`1.6.x`) of the BitShares network — their signing account, last produced block, missed blocks and total votes. It's a read-only directory; you cast your actual votes on the [Vote](/docs/governance/vote.html) page.

**Open in the app:** [Witnesses](/witnesses.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Producer list" icon="eye">
    All witnesses with their stats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Health" icon="shield">
    Missed blocks and last-seen time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Votes" icon="gavel">
    Total votes each witness has received.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Witnesses** from the homepage under *Governance*.

2. Browse the list of witnesses and their performance.

3. To actually vote for one, open [Vote](/docs/governance/vote.html) and select it.

</Steps>
