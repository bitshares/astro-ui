**Ticket Leaderboard** ranks accounts by their effective voting power — the BTS they've locked into tickets, multiplied by each ticket's lock-duration boost. It's a read-only view of who wields the most governance influence.

**Open in the app:** [Ticket Leaderboard](/ticket_leaderboard.html).

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Power ranking" icon="trophy">
    Accounts sorted by effective voting weight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ticket detail" icon="document">
    Inspect any ticket's lock type and amount.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Copy JSON" icon="copy">
    Copy a ticket's raw object for reference.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use

<Steps>

1. Open **Ticket Leaderboard** from the homepage under *Governance*.

2. Browse the ranked list of accounts by voting power.

3. Open a ticket to see its lock type, amount and JSON.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="Earn your place">
  To appear on the leaderboard, lock BTS into a ticket via [Create Ticket](/docs/governance/create_ticket.html).
</Aside>
