## Operations broadcast by this section

- `account_update` — cast your votes.
- `witness_create` / `witness_update` — register/update a witness.
- `committee_member_create` / `committee_member_update` — register/update a committee member.
- `worker_create` — propose a funded worker.
- `ticket_create` / `ticket_update` — lock BTS tickets.

Witnesses, Committee and Ticket Leaderboard are read-only viewers.
